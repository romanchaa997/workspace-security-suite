## Certification Program

### Level 1: Practitioner
- Duration: 2-3 weeks
- Topics: Platform basics, incident response, user management
- Assessment: Multiple choice (80% passing)
- Maintenance: Annual recertification

### Level 2: Specialist
- Prerequisite: Level 1 certification
- Duration: 4-6 weeks
- Topics: Advanced analytics, compliance, custom integrations
- Assessment: Hands-on lab + written exam
- Maintenance: Annual recertification

### Level 3: Expert
- Prerequisite: Level 2 certification
- Duration: 8-12 weeks
- Topics: Architecture, performance tuning, security hardening
- Assessment: Capstone project + comprehensive exam
- Maintenance: Biennial recertification
